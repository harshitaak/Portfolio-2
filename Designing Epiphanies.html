Designing Epiphanies: Harnessing Spontaneity at Will

Project type: Relativistic Conceptualization in Integrative Thinking.

Summation of a thought process in text, and visualization.

This project was the culmination of various chains of mental discourse, pooled up in the "Epiphanetic Design" thought school, at International Open Elective-18, National Institute of Design.

20 students from various backgrounds were part of the deliberations, that delved into the possibility and validation through self-experience, of harnessing the subconscious at will, and learning to program our own minds to better reveal the true nature of our own interactions with our environment, and its effect on self.

While the solutions arrived upon were too particularistic to be listed here, the nature of THE Question itself reveals much to the probing eye and hence is presented here, as an abstract elaboration, with a Graphic designed to ask The Question.
